About Us

Our mission at Akyazı Sailing Club is to reconnect the people of Trabzon—a historic maritime city—with the sea.

We aim to make the blue waters of Trabzon accessible to everyone by embracing the power of the wind and the harmony of nature.

Our Activities

  • Professional Sailing Trainings
  • Competitive Sailing Regattas
  • Social Events for Sea Lovers
