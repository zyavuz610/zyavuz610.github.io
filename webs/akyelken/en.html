Our Mission and Activities

We are here to encourage the people of Trabzon, a maritime city, to build a closer bond with the sea.

Discovering the power of nature and the wind, we set sail into the blue. We organize activities suitable for all levels within our club:

Professional Sailing Courses: Learn the fundamentals of sailing with expert instructors. Amateur Seaman Training Document
Exciting Competitions: Experience the thrill of competition in the fierce waters of the Black Sea.
Social Meetings: Become part of a large family that shares a love for the sea.
