Our Mission & Activities

We are here to encourage the people of Trabzon—a historic maritime city—to form a closer bond with the sea.

We set sail to the blue waters by discovering the power of nature and wind. We organize events for all levels within our club:

Professional Sailing Trainings: Learn the fundamentals of seamanship with expert instructors.
Exciting Regattas: Experience competition in the wild waters of the Black Sea.
Social Gatherings: Become part of a large family sharing the love of the sea.
